# 3 Day Online Masterclass on Qualitative Research

![Qualitative Research Masterclass Flyer](/static/images/Qualitative%20workshop.jpeg)

SMS Research Forum, School of Management Studies, CUSAT is organizing a **three-day online masterclass on qualitative research** for researchers, scholars, and faculty members who want a practical introduction to contemporary qualitative methods.

## Schedule

- **Dates:** 26, 27 & 28 March 2026
- **Time:** 02.00 PM - 04.00 PM
- **Mode:** Online via Zoom

## Resource Persons

**Dr. Akanksha Jaiswal**  
Assistant Professor  
Loyola Institute of Business Administration (LIBA)

**Dr. Devi Soumyaja**  
Assistant Professor  
School of Management Studies, CUSAT

## Key Topics

- Introduction to Qualitative Research
- Grounded Theory
- Gioia's methodology
- Should I use Qualitative software?
- Practical application
- Hands-on training
- Publishing Qualitative Research
